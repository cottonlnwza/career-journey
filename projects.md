# Projects

This section contains the projects I have worked on in academic, personal, and technical areas.

## 2026

- [Invoice Management System](projects/DATABASE/README.md) — Full-stack invoice management system built with React 18, Node.js/Express, PostgreSQL, and Docker. Covers customers, products, invoices, receipts, and reports (AI is also being utilized in the work).
- [Boxing Robot](projects/BOXING-ROBOT/README.md) — 3-wheel Omni combat robot with PS5 Bluetooth control, 2-axis arm, and semi-auto attack sequence. Team leader — designed all wiring diagrams and board layout. Production Technology coursework. Grade A.
- [FPGA Pong Game](projects/FPGA-PONG/README.md) — Real-time Pong game implemented in Verilog on Basys 3 Artix-7 FPGA. VGA controller (640×480 @ 60Hz), collision detection FSM, 7-segment score display. CPE222 coursework. · [Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Is-U1CZ4i60)

- [Travel Planning — Algorithm Comparison](projects/TRAVEL-PLANNING/README.md) — Compared 5 algorithms (Brute Force, DP, Greedy, Genetic Algorithm, PSO) on a 0/1 Knapsack travel problem in Python. All converged to the same optimal: 6 cities in 14 days at $334. CPE231 coursework.

## 2025

- [Mushroom Detection Prototype](projects/MUSHROOM-DETECTION/README.md) — Automatic mushroom inspection prototype using YOLOv8 + DeepSort on Raspberry Pi with LINE Bot alerts. Responsible for full mechanical design and enclosure in SolidWorks. CPE101 group project.
